This assignment was locked Jun 5 at 11:59pm.

Getting Started

To participate in this assignment, you will need to go to the Groups section of this course. To do that, click on People in the menu on the left. Once on the People page, click on Groups. You should be able to see groups titled Homework 9 group followed by a number. You need to sign up to one of these groups (you'll need to scroll down past the groups for the previous assignment, which can be a little slow).  Do not create some other group. Size of each group should be no more than five.

Within your group there are different tools available - you must use your group's Files, Discussions and Collaborations tools for communicating and collaborating with your group members.

Communication tip: don't try to do all your communicating within a single Discussion - make separate Discussions for individual topics. The Collaborations section allows you to create a Google Doc to work on together.

What you must do

Contemplate on the following questions (This is in the context of Question 1 of this week's assignment):

    1. How to prove a problem to be NP-complete. Write the steps based on your discussion.
    2. How are these steps were applied to 3SAT problem in the exploration. 
    3. Reflect how would you apply the steps that you have listed to prove Question 1 in the Assignment of the week. 

Do the following (Check canvas calendar for exact due dates):

  1. By Saturday (3rd June,  11:59PM PST) - Make an individual post on your group page about your answers to the questions or something that's confusing you while you come up these answers.
  2. By Due date - Respond to at least one of the posts made by your group members
  3. As a group come up with the final answers to these questions. 

The intention of this assignment is to enable you to learn to prove NP-complete in a collaborative setting. 

You are allowed to discuss with other groups and refer to other resources, but you must cite them. 

If a group member contributes little or nothing, let the instructor know and they will look at the group's Files, Discussions and Collaborations to verify, and then lower that member's score as appropriate.

Criteria

The submitted report should answer:

  1. Write the steps to prove a problem to be NP-Complete.
  2. Write briefly how would you apply these steps to answer Question 1 of the assignment. (For example, if reducing problem A to problem B is one of your step; discuss how would you do this for Question 1 of the assignment. Briefly write about it). Note that you would be writing the complete proof in the Assignment individually based on your discussion with your group.

List the names of all members of your group at the top. Once the group has finished, only one person needs to submit the pdf in Canvas on behalf of the group.

Submission Details

The Group Part must be submitted as a pdf here in Canvas.

Evaluation

  1. Write the steps to prove a problem to be NP-Complete. [5 points]
  2. Write briefly how would you apply these steps to answer Question 1 of the assignment. [5 points]
